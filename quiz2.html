Quiz 2: Strong Passwords

What makes a password strong?

  • Using only letters
  • Using a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Using a common word or phrase

Why is it important to keep your password secret?

  • So you can remember it easily
  • So others can't access your account
  • So you can share it with friends

What is an example of a weak password?

  • Giraffe#88Lemon
  • ilovecats
  • Password123
