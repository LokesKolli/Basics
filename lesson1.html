Lesson 1: Introduction to Security

Welcome to CyberSafe Kids!

Cybersecurity is like protecting your toys and personal belongings from others. Just like you lock your toy box or keep your room door closed, you need to protect your online identity and personal information from others.

Why is cybersecurity important? Well, there are bad people out there who might try to steal your personal information or hack into your accounts. But don't worry, we'll teach you how to stay safe and have fun online!
